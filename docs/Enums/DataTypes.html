DataTypes

public enum DataTypes

The labeled types below are currently supported in SwiftDBC. The types ‘Array’, DataLink, Distinct, JavaObject, Ref, RefCursor, RowId, SqlXml, and Struct are mostly Oracle specific and don’t have an equivalence in other databases. They are not currently supported but may be in the future because of the HUGE popularity of Oracle.
